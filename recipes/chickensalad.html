This salad works as a light meal or a side dish, offering a satisfying combination of textures and flavors. It's easy to customize, making it ideal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  1. Romaine lettuce
  2. Caesar dressing
  3. Croutons
  4. Parmesan cheese
  5. Olive oil
  6. Salt
  7. Pepper

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in olive oil over medium heat until golden and cooked through.
  2. Slice the cooked chicken into strips.
  3. Tear the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces and place in a large bowl.
  4. Add the sliced chicken, Caesar dressing, croutons, and Parmesan cheese to the lettuce. Toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan if desired.
