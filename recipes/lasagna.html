Lasagna Recipe

Homepage

This lasagna is a timeless classic that combines rich layers of savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melty mozzarella. The sauce is made with a hearty mix of ground beef and Italian sausage, slow-cooked with onions, garlic, and a blend of Italian herbs like basil and oregano. These flavors meld together beautifully, creating a rich, comforting base for the dish.

The layers are stacked with care, alternating between the meat sauce, pasta, and cheese, ensuring every bite is full of flavor. Whether you’re cooking for a family gathering or making a batch to enjoy over a few days, this lasagna is sure to impress. It's simple to make, yet incredibly satisfying, and the perfect meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  1. Ground beef
  2. Italian sausage
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic
  5. Canned tomatoes
  6. Tomato paste and sauce
  7. Sugar
  8. Dried basil
  9. Oregano
  10. Salt
  11. Black pepper
  12. Fresh parsley
  13. Lasagna noodles
  14. Ricotta cheese
  15. Egg
  16. Mozzarella cheese
  17. Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until softened.
  5. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and a bit of water. Add sugar, basil, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and parsley. Mix well.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, followed by more sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until the dish is full, ending with a top layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  10. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
